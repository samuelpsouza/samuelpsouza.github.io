About me

I am a software developer who loves to code with a demonstrated history of working in information technology. Through coding, I contribute to better products so people can perform their tasks gracefully. In my opinion, to achieve such a contribution it is necessary to provide guarantees that the code works and works correctly, and my approach to providing those is writing unit tests, integration tests, thinking about the impact each instruction will have on the whole application and have the mindset that it can be better, following the best software engineering practices.

From my internship to the actual job I have mainly worked with Java, which leads me to be skilled in Spring Boot. In addition, I have worked with Spring Cloud, Redis(used as cache and message queue), Kafka, Maven, Jenkins, and React. Also, I have done a few DevOps tasks, such as deploying tomcat servers to both GCP and AWS, configuring in loco SonarQube, deploying containers to Marathon, and several ones.
